Ivy Tech Community College created the Center for Cyber Security to meet the demands for high-quality cyber security
education and training. Through our nationally recognized center, Ivy Tech students receive the education and training they
need for jobs that secure our community, state and nation’s computers, networks and critical infrastructure.

The program will build the foundationto identify and fix computer security attacks. The material covered in the program is
designed for students with, advanced computer or currently working in the computer industry. Cyber Security is a

stronger Cyber Security with the increasing media attention.'CHAR(13), CHAR(10),
